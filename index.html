Save the koalas

Play video

These amazing creatures need your help more than ever!

Throughout the years wildfires in Australia have caused tremendous damage to the forest that is home to a diverse wildlife. The most recent wildfires have contributed to the death of many animals - especially koalas. Koalas may become extinct.

Donation so far $0

According to an article from the New York Times "the International Union for Conservation of Nature’s Red List, the authority on the conservation status of the world’s species, says the koala population is declining and vulnerable". Together we can save the koalas and help restore their population and ecosystem.
